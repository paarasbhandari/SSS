About us



The Space and Satellite Systems Club at UC Davis aims to provide a hands-on experience for students that will prepare them for a future career in the aerospace industry. In the past, we’ve competed in the CanSat competition hosted by AIAA and AAS and began our own competition at UC Davis.



This year, we will be expanding the CubeSat Systems Competition to include other universities as we compete to see who can design and build the best attitude determination and control system. More on this can be found on the CubeSat Systems Competition page.
