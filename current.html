Current Project



We are currently developing the first satellite to be designed and built solely by undergraduates at UC Davis. The Quad Amateur Radio Communications Satellite or QuARCS will be testing the efficiency of the Iridium and Globalstar Constellations in addition to investigating the effects of the Doppler Shift that we expect to occur during transmission between the QuARCS satellite and Iridium Constellation.

We are seeking funding for this project, so if you or an organization you represent is able, please contact Anna Rita Moukarzel for more information.
